Pop Music

Pop music, short for popular music, is a genre characterized by its mainstream appeal and widespread accessibility. Known for catchy melodies, relatable lyrics, and simple song structures, pop music often incorporates elements from other musical genres, such as rock, hip-hop, electronic, and dance music.

Indian Music

Indian music is a rich and diverse tradition that includes classical, folk, and popular styles. The two main classical traditions are Hindustani from North India and Carnatic from South India. Indian music features complex melodies and rhythms governed by the raga and tala systems.

K-Pop Music

K-pop, short for Korean pop, is a genre of popular music that originated in South Korea. It blends various musical styles such as pop, hip-hop, R&B, and EDM. K-pop is known for its high-energy performances, catchy melodies, polished production, and elaborate music videos.

Hip-hop Music

Hip-hop is a music genre and cultural movement that started in the Bronx, New York City, in the 1970s. It is known for rapping (spoken word lyrics over a beat) and includes strong, catchy beats using samples and loops. Hip-hop often tells stories or addresses social issues in its lyrics.
